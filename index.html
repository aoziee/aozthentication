About

Cinnamoroll

Hello! <3

Welcome to the enchanting world of Cinnamoroll, where dreams and fluffiness collide! Cinnamoroll is an adorable and endearing character created by Sanrio, a beloved Japanese company known for bringing smiles to people of all ages.


Origins

Cinnamoroll, also known as "Cinnamon," first made his debut in 2001. He is a little white puppy with long ears resembling cinnamon rolls. He hails from the mystical Cafe Cinnamon, a place of wonder and sweetness. Cinnamoroll captured hearts with his innocent charm and the desire to make friends wherever he goes.


Personality

Cinnamoroll is a cheerful and playful pup, always ready for exciting adventures. He loves making new friends and is incredibly friendly and approachable. With a heart as warm as freshly baked cinnamon rolls, Cinnamoroll spreads happiness wherever he goes.


Organization

Join the Cinnamoroll Community Here at the Cinnamoroll website, we celebrate the love for this charming pup. Join us in exploring the magical world of Cinnamoroll, discovering his adventures, merchandise, and more. Let's spread joy and embrace the sweetness together!
